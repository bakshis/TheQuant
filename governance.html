Governance

A Panchayat* will eventually lead this Institute and its members will be selected, not elected. Commitment and  wisdom will be the guiding criteria for selection and will comprise of 3 women
and 2 men.
By the community, Of the Community and For the Community.
Member collaboration (sahayog), teamwork  (samilit) and volunteering  (kar seva ) will be the hallmark of this community.
Governance practices, compliance and ﬁnancial records are transparent.  High professional standards consistent with global best practices are maintained.
Roles and involvement are guided by passion and  interests and skills of its members, matched with the needs of the community.
*Inspired by the ancient tradition of governance in Indian villages; a group of ﬁve wise, respected and inﬂuential elder people, acknowledged by the community as their leaders.
