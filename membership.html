1. Type Of Membership

1.1 Founder Membership
INR 5,00,000 One Time

You are passionate about this initiative and would like to be associated and make a substantial contribution to the growth of the community.

Your kindness will be acknowledged, at prominent places, with gratitude for all times to come. All Founder Members will enjoy Life Member status.

You can nominate one person – spouse, partner or soul mate, who will enjoy complimentary Annual Membership for one year. This can be renewed as per the Annual Membership norms at the start of new ﬁnancial year, each year.

You will be invited for the Annual General Meetings of the Institute and ﬁrst row will be reserved for Founder Members at all events of TQIW.

1.2 Life Membership - INDIVIDUAL
(Limited offer – 100 members) – INR 1,00,000 One Time

You are passionate about this initiative and would like to be associated and are willing to pay a onetime lifelong membership fee, instead of an annual fee.

1.3 Life Membership - COUPLE
INR 1,50,000 One Time

If you as a couple feel motivated to become life members, you can pay an additional amount to acquire the status of couple life membership.

1.4 Annual Membership - INDIVIDUAL
INR 10,000 per year from date of joining

You are passionate about this movement and would like to renew your involvement each year.

We will also accept passionate seekers who cannot afford the fee. Please write to us, requesting for a subsidy and offering whatever your conscious permits, as a fair exchange.

1.5 Annual Membership - COUPLE
INR 15,000 per year from date of joining

If you as a couple feel motivated to become annual members, you can pay an additional amount to acquire the status of couple life membership.

1.6 Youth Membership
INR 5,000 per year from date of joining

Any student who wishes to be part of the community can apply for this special membership. membership.

Please submit your student proof at the time of form ﬁlling to avail the student discount.

2. Categories Of Members

2.1 Allopathic doctors: you are a qualiﬁed allopathic doctor and can by law prescribe medicine in your jurisdiction – e.g. qualiﬁed MD in allopathic medicine

2.2 Doctor / Practitioner: you practice medicine / healing which is regulated but excludes you from prescribing allopathic medicines – e.g. homeopathy, osteopathy

2.3 Therapist: you have a recognized certiﬁcate to practice a therapy or healing modality – e.g. physiotherapy

2.4 Healer: you have a natural gift and have trained to impart healing
