Event Support

TQIW Members who wish to organize micro events to attract other members to attend their programs will be supported by TQIW by sending communications to its members.

Such events should conﬁrm to the purpose and role of TQIW.

In the community, they are members who are willing to host such micro events on a pro bono basis and may offer the use of their space for such events.

Responsibility for meeting the expenses, if any, for the event is that of the member.
