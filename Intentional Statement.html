about us
Intentional Statement The Quantum Institute for Wellbeing(TQIW) is an Intentional Community of seekers in search of Holistic and Integrative approach for personal wellbeing.
On one hand the community comprises of Doctors and Practitioners in the quest of improving their healing therapies and aiming for better results for their clients; and on the other hand, individuals who wish to pursue practices and therapies which will help them maintain good health and wellbeing.
Both parties recognize the power of Holistic, Integrated and Natural approaches to wellbeing and are enthusiastic to learn and apply the knowledge that is being rediscovered as change in consciousness around the world.
TQIW is a knowledge sharing and learning platform. Its committed to attract the best resources around the globe.
It invests in due diligence processes to assure authenticity and to bring the best perspectives to its members to enable them to deploy insights to enhance their life journeys.
It inspires businesses in the wellbeing space to improve their "business modules and value propositions" and take advantage of the new sunrise opportunity on
the horizon.
