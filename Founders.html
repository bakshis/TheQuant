Anuroop ( Tony) Singh

Anuroop (Tony) Singh is the Founder Director & promoter of The Quantum Institute for Wellbeing, an Intentional community promoting Holistic and Integrated approaches to Wellbeing (www.tqiw.org) , a personal endeavor to give back to humanity. He qualiﬁed as a Chartered Accountant, has travelled widely and currently consults with socially responsible organizations across India, UAE, Europe and USA. He is an experienced business leader and has been an independent member on the Board of several leading companies. He has more than four decades of executive experience in the ﬁnancial services sector, including two decades as Regional Chief Executive for various businesses including American Express, Bank of America, ANZ Grindlays Bank and New York Life. He has over 15 years of  governance experience on the boards of several listed companies including Perot Systems, USA(www.perotsystems.com); Max India (www.maxindia.com) ; Max Bupa (www.maxbupa.com);  Max New York Life (www.maxlifeinsurance.com) ; Development Credit Bank (www.dcbbank.com) and served in various capacities as Chairman, Vice Chairman, and head of key  committees including  Risk, Audit and Human Resources . Members

Founder Members are those members who have participated actively in building the foundation of The Quantum Institute for Wellbeing, from its inception.
All seekers who wish to actively participate in building up the community and be recognized as Founder Members are encouraged to join in this category of Membership.
The Founder Members are recognized prominently at the Institute and published in its catalogues and key communications by a special mention.
